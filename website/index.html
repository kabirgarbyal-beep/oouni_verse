Frequently Asked Questions

Standard custom orders typically take 8-12 business days from design confirmation. Urgent orders can be expedited for an additional fee, subject to yarn availability and artisan schedule.

All our yarn is 100% organic Coimbatore cotton, sourced from sustainable farms in South India. We never use synthetic fibers, acrylics, or chemically treated yarns.

Gentle hand wash in cold water with mild detergent, lay flat to dry in shade. Avoid machine washing, bleach, or direct sunlight. Iron on low heat if needed.
